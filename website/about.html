About TECHIES TECH TIPS

TECHIES TECH TIPS was founded by Landen Darling. What started as simply fixing computers for himself and his dad quickly grew into something more. As his skills expanded into PC building and repairing smaller electronics like phones, he discovered a passion for solving technology problems. In 2024, he completed his first commissioned repairs, which sparked the ambition to build something bigger. That moment helped inspire the creation of TECHIES TECH TIPS — a place where people can get reliable help with their technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make technology easier, more reliable, and more accessible for everyone. Whether it’s fixing a broken computer, building a powerful PC, or helping someone understand their devices, TECHIES TECH TIPS exists to simplify technology.

Meet the Founder

Landen Darling is the founder and owner of TECHIES TECH TIPS. His passion for technology started with repairing personal computers and quickly grew into PC building and electronics troubleshooting. Today he continues helping people fix, improve, and understand their technology.
